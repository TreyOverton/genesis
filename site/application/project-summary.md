# Project Summary (Abstract)

> **Status:** Not started · **Owner:** Unassigned

Attached to Field 7 of the R&R Other Project Information form.

## Requirements

- **Page limit:** 1 page of content (project title and investigator list don't count toward the limit).
- **Format:** 8.5 × 11, 1-inch margins, body ≥ 11 pt.
- **Self-contained** — sufficient for reviewers to identify conflicts of interest.
- **Public.** If funded, DOE may use this abstract in public reports describing the award.
- **No proprietary or sensitive business information.**

## Required contents

1. Project title, **application stage (Phase I)**, PI name and institutional affiliation, all co-investigators and their affiliations. *(Does not count toward the 1-page limit.)*
2. Statement of the project's **vision** for the science/applied-energy initiatives and a description of **how and why AI** will enhance the associated scientific and technical workflows.
3. Description of the proposed research (≤ 1 page, excluding title and investigator list). Figures and references, if included, must fit within the 1-page limit.
