# Appendix 2: Facilities & Other Resources

> **Status:** Not started · **Owner:** Unassigned

Part of the single Project Narrative PDF. **Does not count** toward the 5-page narrative limit.

## Limits

- **Page limit:** ≤ 1 page (8.5 × 11, 1-inch margins).
- **Do not attach to Field 10** of the R&R Other Project Information form.

## Required contents

- Identify resources/facilities to be used (Laboratory, Animal, Computer, Office, Clinical, Other).
- Capacities, pertinent capabilities, relative proximity, extent of availability to the project.
- Describe **only** resources directly applicable to the proposed work.
- **Computing, networking, and data resources** required for the proposed work.
  - DOE may have computing resources available from DOE/NNSA National Laboratories and/or private-sector Genesis Mission partners.
  - Describe current computing allocations that will be used, or intent to apply for allocations.
  - **Same computational resource estimate also goes** in the Genesis Mission Phase I Application Template (Excel) attached to Field 12.
- Other necessary resources (machine shop, electronic shop, etc.) and extent of availability.
- **Experimental user facilities at other institutions:** include a document from the facility manager confirming researchers will have access. Such documents may be printed on any letterhead but must **not** become letters of support/recommendation.
