# Appendix 7: Letters of Commitment & Collaboration

> **Status:** Not started · **Owner:** Unassigned

Part of the single Project Narrative PDF. **Does not count** toward the 5-page narrative limit.

## Letters of Commitment — REQUIRED in specific cases

Per the RFA component pieces guidance:

- **Funded partners** providing resources (facilities, data, services).
- **Industrial partners** — *strongly encouraged* for focus area 1-D (Digitalization of Industrial Processes); include commitment letters if an industry partner is on the team.
- Human/animal subjects, select agents, or similar regulated work — commitment letters from oversight bodies as applicable.

## Letters of Collaboration or Access — OPTIONAL

- From collaborators who contribute to the project without receiving funds.
- **Experimental user facility access:** documents from facility managers confirming access for research team members (referenced from [Appendix 2](appendix-2-facilities.md)). These may be printed on any letterhead but must **not** be letters of support/recommendation.
