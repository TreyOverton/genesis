# Appendix 5: Synergistic Activities (Optional)

> **Status:** Optional · **Owner:** Unassigned

Part of the single Project Narrative PDF. **Does not count** toward the 5-page narrative limit.

## Purpose

In addition to biographical sketches (which live on the Senior/Key Person Profile form), each senior/key person **has the option** to provide a list of up to **5 distinct examples** of synergistic activities that demonstrate their professional and scholarly work focused on the integration, transfer, and creation of knowledge as related to the application.

## Limits

- **Per senior/key person:** 1 page with ≤ 5 examples.
- **Appendix limit:** same number of pages as senior/key personnel (i.e., one page per participating person).
- **Do not attach separately** to Field 12 of the R&R Other Project Information form.
