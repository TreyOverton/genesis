# DOE Title Page

> **Status:** Not started · **Owner:** Unassigned

Front matter of the single Project Narrative PDF (attached to Field 8). **Does not count** toward the 5-page narrative limit.

## Required items

- Project title
- Lead Applicant / Institution
- Street Address (street, city, state, ZIP)
- Postal Address (if different from street)
- Lead PI name, telephone, email
- Administrative Point of Contact name, telephone, email
- RFA Number: **DE-FOA-0003612**
- Application Stage: **Phase I**
- Primary challenge and focus area per [Section III](../rfa/pages/3-program-description.md): **2 (Scaling the Biotechnology Revolution) | E (AI-Enabled Biological Reaction Engineering, Bioreactor Design, Process Scale-up and Integration)**

## Genesis Mission Phase I Application Template (Excel)

DOE provides an Excel template on the RFA's Grants.gov page ("Related Documents" tab) that compiles senior/key personnel, partner institutions, budgets, computational resource estimate, and focus area across all applications. **Attach the completed Excel to Field 12** of the R&R Other Project Information form. Use this template — it's required for uniformity.

## PDF assembly rules

The single narrative PDF (Title Page + Project Narrative + Appendices) must be:

- Machine-readable — **not scanned** from a printed document
- No editable fields, signatures, passwords, or redactions
- No PDF portfolios or binders
- Combined via "Print to PDF" or equivalent
- Readable in Adobe Acrobat

Do not attach appendices separately to Fields 9, 10, 11, or 12 (with the exception of the Excel template to Field 12).
