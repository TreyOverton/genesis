# Appendix 4: Data Management and Sharing Plan (DMSP)

> **Status:** Not required at submission · **Owner:** Unassigned

> **Phase I:** A DMSP is **NOT required** as an appendix to the Project Narrative at submission, but **will be required during award negotiation**. Recommended to draft early.

## Cost note

Subject to applicable cost principles, applications may request costs necessary for implementing the DMSP.

## Genesis-specific requirements

The DMSP must specifically address:

- **FAIR principles** (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable) applied to anticipated datasets, software, and models.
- **Open-source licensing** of developed software, datasets, and models — note the [SPDX identifier(s)](https://spdx.org/licenses/) when possible. Where expected to deviate from [The Open Source Initiative's Open Source Definition](https://opensource.org/osd), provide specific justification.
- **Best practices in scientific software development.** See [Better Scientific Software](https://bssw.io/).

## Standard SC DMSP requirements

See [IX.B.12 How to Prepare a Data Management and Sharing Plan](../rfa/pages/9-other-information.md) for baseline DOE Office of Science DMSP requirements.
