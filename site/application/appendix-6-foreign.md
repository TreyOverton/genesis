# Appendix 6: Transparency of Foreign Connections

> **Status:** Not started · **Owner:** Unassigned

Part of the single Project Narrative PDF. **Does not count** toward the 5-page narrative limit.

## Scope

**One disclosure per entity.** Separate disclosures required for the applicant **and each proposed subrecipient**.

## Format

A template is available at "Transparency of Foreign Connections" (referenced in the RFA). Use of this template is **not required** — if using another format, the signatory must include the same substantive information, a signature, the date, and the certification statement from the RFA template.

## Disclosure exceptions by entity type

- **U.S. national laboratories and domestic government entities** — disclosure **not required**.
- **Institutions of higher education** — only required to respond to items with an asterisk (*).
- **All other project team members** — complete responses required.
- Disclosure requirements are determined by entity type. Even if the applicant is exempt, subrecipients must provide disclosures unless they are also exempt.
