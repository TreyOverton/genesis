# Appendix 1: Bibliography & References Cited

> **Status:** Not started · **Owner:** Unassigned

Part of the single Project Narrative PDF. **Does not count** toward the 5-page narrative limit.

## Requirements

- Include every reference cited in the Project Narrative.
- Each reference: all author names (in publication order), article/journal title, book title, volume, pages, year.
- For research areas with routinely > 10 coauthors, abbreviated styles are allowed (e.g., Physical Review Letters convention — first author *et al.*).
- **Bibliographic citations only.**
- **Do not attach to Field 9** of the R&R Other Project Information form — include as an appendix in the single narrative PDF.
